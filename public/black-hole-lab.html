Equations

Simplified educational approximations:

Rs = 2GM / c²
t_far = t_local / sqrt(1 - Rs/r)
v_escape = sqrt(2GM/r)
photon sphere = 1.5 Rs
ISCO ≈ 3 Rs for a non-spinning black hole
tidal scale ∝ M / r³

Hawking Radiation
