Telescope Integration

Connect StellaSim to your telescope and use the Live Star Map as a real observing control center.

  1. 1Tap a star, planet, nebula, galaxy, or Moon feature in the Live Star Map.
  2. 2StellaSim reads the target position and prepares the telescope movement.
  3. 3Use GoTo control to send the telescope toward the object and keep your session organized.
