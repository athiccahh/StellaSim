Dark Adaptation Guide

Small habits that make a real difference outside.

Protect your night vision

Avoid white light, use dim red light, and give your eyes 20-30 minutes to adapt.

Block nearby lights

Use a hood, shield, building shadow, or car as a light block when streetlights are nearby.

Time it right

Observe faint galaxies and nebulae after astronomical twilight and away from bright moonlight.
